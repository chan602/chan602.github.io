What are the Three Wins?

Every day I try to get three wins — a physical, mental, and spiritual win. It's an idea that's circulated online for a while, and I wanted to try it because I value all three categories and want to be intentional about them. The parameters are broad and personal — what counts as a spiritual win for me might be completely different for someone else. It boils down to hitting three themes that make me feel well, accomplished, and happy at the end of the day. More than just being productive. Kind of like Maslow's hierarchy of needs, but as a daily checklist.

For years I was a sticky note todo list guy. I switched to tracking tasks in ChatGPT thinking the AI could also evaluate my three wins — but todo lists are rigid algorithmic logic, and AI isn't well suited to maintain them. Tasks got misinterpreted, forgotten, inconsistently tracked. It wasn't working. But I still wanted the AI evaluation piece.

So I built this. A rigid, logical todo system with AI coming in afterwards to evaluate — not to manage. After a lot of iterative effort, debugging, and optimization, I have a system that actually works for me, with daily and weekly tasks archived and evaluated by the Anthropic API. I'm really proud of this build, and I'm working on scaling it into something I could use with friends. Thanks for checking it out!
