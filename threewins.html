What are the Three Wins?

The Three Wins system is a daily practice of intentionally achieving one physical, one mental, and one spiritual win each day. The idea has roots in performance psychology, stoic philosophy, and the routines of high performers — from athletes to surgeons to entrepreneurs. The premise is simple: if you win in all three dimensions every day, you are living a complete and disciplined life.

Physical — any meaningful movement. A climb session, a run, a workout, MMA training. Your body is your most important tool.
Mental — academic, professional, or goal-directed work. Studying, researching, building, solving. Feed your mind deliberately.
Spiritual — the broadest category. Journaling, meditation, prayer, sleeping early, meaningful conversation, reflection. Anything that reconnects you to purpose and presence.
